Grilled Splendor Squid

June 2, 2023

DescriptionIngredientCooking MethodPrice

Ingredient

  • Olive Oil (2 tbsp)
  • Fresh Squid (300g)
  • Lemon Juice (1 lemon)
  • Fresh Garlic (3 cloves, minced)
  • Fresh Parsley (1 tbsp, chopped)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black Pepper (to taste)

Cooking Method

  1. Firstly, our chef handpicks fresh, locally-sourced squid, ensuring the finest quality. Each piece is meticulously cleaned, preparing it for the symphony of flavors to come.
  2. Next, the squid is bathed in a specially crafted marinade, a blend of extra virgin olive oil, aromatic minced garlic, zesty lemons, hand-chopped parsley, and a perfect balance of salt and pepper.
  3. This marinade, soaked up by the squid for two hours, enhances its natural flavor, setting the stage for a true culinary masterpiece.
  4. The squid is then expertly grilled at just the right temperature, turning it a beautiful translucent white.
  5. Our chef's secret touch? Basting the squid throughout the grilling process with the leftover marinade for an extra infusion of flavor.
  6. Lastly, after grilling, the squid is given a brief moment to rest. This allows the flavors to settle and intensify, ensuring each bite is a taste sensation.
