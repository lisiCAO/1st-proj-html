Grilled Splendor Squid

June 2, 2023

Description | Ingredient | Cooking Method | Price

Ingredient

  • Olive Oil (2 tbsp)
  • Fresh Squid (300g)
  • Lemon Juice (1 lemon)
  • Fresh Garlic (3 cloves, minced)
  • Fresh Parsley (1 tbsp, chopped)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black Pepper (to taste)

Cooking Method

  1. Thoroughly clean the fresh squid and pat dry.
  2. Mix olive oil, minced garlic, fresh lemon juice, chopped parsley, salt, and black pepper in a bowl to make the marinade.
  3. Marinate the squid in the mixture for about 2 hours to absorb the flavors.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Grill each side of the squid for about 2-3 minutes or until the squid turns a translucent white color, basting with the leftover marinade for additional flavor.
  6. Remove the squid from the grill and let it rest for a minute before serving.
